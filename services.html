Branding

Creatif Fundi offers both corporate and product branding solutions. Branding is the "personality" of a company, product, service or self.

Corporate branding - the promotion of the brand name of a corporate entity.

This goes beyond the branding of specific products or services. The activities and thinking that go into corporate branding are different from product and service branding because the scope of a corporate brand is typically much broader.
